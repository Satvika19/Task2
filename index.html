Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

The man who saved a billion lives

Chhatrapati Shivaji, was an Indian ruler and a member of the Bhonsle Maratha clan.

Here's a time line of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj:

  • 1627 - Born in Ahmadnagar Sultanate, Shivneri Fort
  • Before age of 20 - Shivaji entered into military career
  • 1661 - the Sultan of Bijapur made peace with Shivaji and recognized his conquests.
  • 1664 - Shivaji attacked and plundered Surat, the richest port on the West coast.
  • 1674 - Shivaji was crowned as King
  • 1680 - Shivaji died

"Shivaji Raje Bhosle with title of Shivaji Maharaj founded Maratha empire. He successfully fought with Mughals and remained a challenge for Auranzeb"
